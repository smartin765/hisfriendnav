About historyfriend.com

Historyfriend is a educational website for elementary learners, parents and teachers. It is intended as a history research website. After working as an elemetary homeroom teacher for several years I created Historyfriend as a resource which provides printable info about historical figures which is graded for elementary students. There are over 80 historical profiles, printable sheets, pictures and videos to help you learn about history! If you find any errors please do not hesitate to contact me at: historyfriend8@gmail.com
